# 2025 Ecosystem Working Group Steward Election, Term 6

::author

This is a vote to elect three stewards for the Ecosystem Working Group for the Term of calendar 2025 (ENS DAO Term 6). Term 6, the only term of 2025, commences at 9am UTC on January 1, 2025.

Elections are conducted by approval voting. Approval voting allows you to select all the candidates you support for the role of Working Group steward.

To vote, select each candidate you approve for the role of steward.

You can select as many or as few candidates to approve as you like.

At the conclusion of voting, the three highest ranked candidates will be elected as stewards of the Ecosystem Working Group for Term 6, calendar 2025 year.

A list of candidates, with links to their nomination statement and Twitter profile, is included below. The list is presented in the order in which candidates submitted their nomination in the [ENS DAO governance forum](https://discuss.ens.domains/t/ecosystem-working-group-steward-nominations-for-term-6-calendar-2025/19911).
