# Special Election: Meta-Governance Working Group Steward (Term 5 Vacancy)

::author

This is a special election vote to fill one vacant steward position in the Meta-Governance Working Group following the resignation of Avsa.eth. Per the Working Group Rules, this election will fill the vacant position for the remainder of Term 5 (2024).

Elections are conducted by approval voting. Approval voting allows you to select all the candidates you support for the role of Working Group steward.

To vote:

- Select each candidate you approve for the role of steward
- You can select as many or as few candidates to approve as you like
- At the conclusion of voting, the highest ranked candidate will be elected as steward of the Meta-Governance Working Group for the remainder of Term 5

A list of candidates, with links to their nomination statement and Twitter profile, is included below. The list is presented in the order in which candidates submitted their nomination in the ENS DAO governance forum.
